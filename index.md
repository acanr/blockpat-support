## FAQ

**Q: How do I play?**
Drag blocks from the tray onto the 8×8 grid. Fill complete rows or columns to clear them and score points.

**Q: What is Adventure Mode?**
Adventure Mode takes you through atmospheric journeys, each with its own visual world. As you progress through levels, themes evolve — from misty valleys to ancient golden trees. New worlds unlock as you complete previous ones.

**Q: What is Endless Mode?**
Endless Mode is the classic experience — no levels, no themes changing, just pure puzzle. Keep playing as long as you can and chase your high score.

**Q: Can I save my progress in Adventure Mode?**
Yes! Your progress is saved automatically whenever you leave the game. Even if you close the app completely, you can return right where you left off — your grid, pieces, level and score all preserved.

**Q: How do coins work?**
When you clear lines, coins appear on your blocks. Clear the rows or columns where coins sit to collect them. Use coins for Undo or Continue.

**Q: What is Undo?**
Undo lets you take back your last move. You get 1 free undo per day, or spend 40 coins.

**Q: How does Continue work?**
After Game Over, you can continue by watching an ad (once per game) or spending coins. You'll receive a Bomb and 2 single blocks — place the Bomb to blast a 3×3 area and keep playing.

**Q: What is the Combo system?**
Clear lines back-to-back to chain combos: COMBO → x2 GREAT → x3 AMAZING → FANTASTIC → LEGENDARY! Higher combos earn more points.

**Q: How do I double my coins?**
After Game Over, tap "Double Coins" to watch a short ad and double what you earned that game. This is always optional.

**Q: How do I unlock new worlds?**
Complete the final level of an Adventure world to unlock the next one. Each world has its own progression and best score that's saved separately.

**Q: Is internet required?**
No. BlockPat! works fully offline.

**Q: Are there forced ads?**
No. All ads in BlockPat! are optional and rewarded — you choose to watch them for bonuses. No banners, no pop-ups.

**Q: Is the game free?**
Yes! BlockPat! is completely free to play.

**Q: How is my data used?**
BlockPat! does not collect or store any personal data. Your high scores, coins, and Adventure progress are saved locally on your device only.
