## FAQ

**Q: How do I play?**
Drag blocks from the tray onto the 8×8 grid. Fill complete rows or columns to clear them and score points.

**Q: What are special blocks?**
💣 Bomb clears a 3×3 area. ⚡ Lightning clears an entire row and column instantly.

**Q: Is internet required?**
No. BlockPat! works fully offline.

**Q: How do I continue after Game Over?**
Watch a short ad to receive a bonus bomb and keep playing. You can use this once per game.

**Q: Is the game free?**
Yes! BlockPat! is completely free to play.

**Q: How is my data used?**
BlockPat! does not collect or store any personal data. Your high score is saved locally on your device only.
